Recipe #1: Porkchops with Pan Sauce

Five Awesome Recipes Accessible for Everyone

Hello! My name is Jaeden and this is my website! Ever since I was a child, I have enjoyed eating food and the amazing experiences that come with it. From sharing food with my extended family at big occasions, to the small times where we would get a bucket of Fried Chicken for lunch or for dinner. Now, in the time of the pandemic, I feel that being able to experience good food that is both easy to cook and cheap is a necessity, especially for budding home cooks like myself. I will be sharing 5 recipes that fit all of these criteria while also fitting things you can easily get in the grocery store.
